Call for Papers

The 9th International KES Conference Smart Education and E-Learning (SEEL 2022) will host a forum for PhD students to demonstrate their dissertation research. The PhD Forum spans various topics of smart pedagogy, smart universities, smart education technologies and systems and related fields such as data processing, statistics, artificial intelligence, data and web mining. Application areas of that topics, such as smart pedagogy are encouraged. Participants with interdisciplinary work across the areas are particularly welcome. The PhD Forum invites papers from the doctoral students describing their research. Accepted papers will be published by Springer as book chapters in a volume of the KES Smart Innovation Systems and Technologies series, submitted for indexing in Scopus and Thomson Reuters Conference Proceedings Citation Index (CPCI) and the Web of Science. During the section, professors with experience in directing and studying doctoral students will take part in choosing the Best Presentation and provide feedback to participants. The best paper will receive the Best Paper Award.

Conference languages: English.

Proceedings language: English.

The conference gives PhD students and scientists a possibility to exchange their experience and results in perspective research and development of smart pedagogy.

Submitted papers must be unpublished and not considered elsewhere for publication. All contributions must be of high quality, original, should not have been published elsewhere and should not be intended for publication elsewhere during the review period or time of the conference. To ensure high quality, all papers will be thoroughly reviewed by the conference Programme Committee. Papers may be rejected without being sent to the reviewers if they do not comply with the required format, length or if they do not lie within the scope of the conference.

Please refer to the registration page to learn more about the registration procedure and the requirements for participation.

Proceedings

The best papers from the main sessions will be selected for publication in Conference Proceedings. The Proceedings will be available in printed and online versions.

We are going to publish the proceedings in Springer with selectable Scopus indexing. For more information, refer to the publication page.

Research Tracks

The research track welcomes novel papers addressing theoretical, applied, and empirical aspects of research and development. Topics of interest include but are not limited to:

Smart pedagogy

  • Best practices and case studies on smart education
  • Smart curriculum and courseware design and development
  • Smart blended, distance, online and open education

Smart education. Methods, technologies ana systems

  • Hardware and software systems, portals and platforms for smart e-learning
  • Smart technology-based educational software and hardware systems
  • Smart gamification (fusion of smart technology and serious games)
  • Smart multimedia (fusion of smart technology and rich multimedia technology)
  • Smart mobility (fusion of smart technology and mobile technology)

Smart universities

  • Concepts of smart university, systems and technologies
  • Design and development outcomes in the field of creating a smart university

The PhD Forum is dedicated to demonstrate works of Ph.D. students focused more on practical and applied tasks. It allows our participants to start international networking of their projects, receive the feedback from experienced researchers.

Best paper award and editorial follow-ups

Papers of special merit will be considered for the selection of the best report in the PhD Forum report competition. The best paper will receive the Best Paper Award.
